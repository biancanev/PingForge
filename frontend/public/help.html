Frequently Asked Questions

How long do webhook sessions last?

Webhook sessions are active for 24 hours by default. You can create new sessions anytime.

Is there a limit on the number of requests?

Free accounts can receive up to 1000 requests per session. Authenticated users get higher limits.

Can I replay captured requests?

Yes! You can forward any captured request to your actual endpoints for testing purposes.
